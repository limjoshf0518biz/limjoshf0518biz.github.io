Commercial Analytics Dashboard Suite

A set of automated dashboards that turned slow, manual reporting into a real-time source of truth — adopted across the cluster for weekly reviews and forecasting.

Role

Category Mgmt Analytics Associate

Context

Shopee Philippines

Stack

SQL · Excel · Power BI

Impact

100% trend accuracy (Jan–Apr)

The problem

Category teams were flying partly blind. Reporting was manual and slow, and there was no granular, real-time view of what actually drove GMV — down to the L2, shop, and SKU level. A leadership review surfaced a critical SKU-level visibility gap that no existing report could answer.

The approach

I built a connected suite of automated dashboards, each solving a specific decision the team faced:

  • GMV Growth Drivers — decomposed performance across L2, shop, and SKU, with out-of-stock tracking to trigger proactive replenishment.
  • Daily Targets Tracker — real-time monitoring of target attainment, enabling timely mid-month interventions.
  • Cluster-wide SOB & Incremental GMV trackers — standardized share-of-business and campaign-uplift reporting across business units.
  • HL Trend Calendar — a seasonality model to forecast top-performing categories ahead of each month.
“The trackers became the primary tool for weekly alignment — and the one we reached for when leadership asked a question no report could yet answer.”

The outcome

The suite cut time-to-insight dramatically and became embedded in recurring business reviews. The SKU-level tracker built during the leadership review is now a standing report. Correcting computation inconsistencies across clusters also improved overall data accuracy and reporting consistency.

Reflection

The lesson that stuck: a dashboard only matters if it changes a decision. The trackers that succeeded were the ones built around a specific question someone needed answered that week — not the ones that simply looked complete.
